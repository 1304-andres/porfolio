About
Mira Okafor Independent · Lisbon + NYC

A designer who thinks in systems and shows up in pixels.

I'm an independent designer with a decade of practice across brand identity, editorial, and interactive product. I partner with founders and art directors to turn abstract positioning into coherent, honest design systems — and the pages, decks, and interfaces that carry them forward.

Previously lead designer at a Brooklyn-based studio where I shipped work for cultural institutions, consumer hardware companies, and a handful of well-behaved crypto teams. Now building my own practice from Lisbon, with residencies in New York each spring.
